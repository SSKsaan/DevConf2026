Frequently Asked Questions

What is the refund and cancellation policy?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. A 50% refund is offered between 14–29 days before the event. Unfortunately, we cannot offer refunds within 14 days of the conference, but tickets are transferable to another person at no cost.

Is there a virtual or remote attendance option?

Yes, a limited number of virtual passes are available. Remote attendees get live-stream access to all keynote sessions and a selection of curated talks, plus access to the digital recording library for 30 days after the event.

Are there group or student discounts available?

Yes, we offer a 15% discount for groups of 5 or more from the same organisation, and a 30% discount for full-time students with a valid .edu email address. Reach out to our team for the promo code and verification process.

What's included in each ticket tier?

The Standard pass includes access to all conference days, 48 technical talks, 2 workshops, networking breaks, and digital recordings. The Pro pass adds unlimited workshops, speaker meet & greet, VIP dinner, lifetime recordings, and a 30-minute 1-on-1 mentorship session.

What dietary and accessibility accommodations are available?

We cater to vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and halal dietary needs at all meal events. The venue is fully wheelchair-accessible with ramps, elevators, and accessible restrooms. Sign-language interpretation and captioning are available upon request — please notify us at least two weeks in advance.

How can I submit questions to the speakers?

Each talk includes a live Q&A session at the end. You can also submit questions in advance through the conference app, which will be curated by session moderators. For 1-on-1 mentorship sessions, you'll have direct discussion time with the speaker.

Will sessions be recorded and available after the conference?

All keynote talks and breakout sessions are professionally recorded. Standard pass holders get 30 days of on-demand access, while Pro pass holders receive lifetime access to the full recording library.
